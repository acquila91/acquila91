This unique app is aimed to simplify and enhance the learning process of trumpet fingering for amateurs and professionals alike. It provides an interesting exercise for users to better associate the sight of a note on the score, its valve position, and the sound it should produce.

App Features:

  • Valve Position Visuals: Instead of an overwhelming chart, the app provides intuitive visuals of which valve to press to produce specific notes, making learning smoother and easier.
  • Customizable Metronome: Set your own tempo according to your comfort and practice pace using the built-in metronome.
  • Range Selection: You can set the lowest and highest notes you wish to play, and the app will tailor the exercises accordingly.
  • Clean User Interface: The user-friendly interface ensures a smooth and uninterrupted learning experience for users of different ages and abilities.

"Music Education Interface - Trumpet" is available for download on both the Apple Store and Google Play Store. This engaging and individualized interface is perfect for teachers looking for innovative teaching materials, trumpet students, and experienced players seeking to refine their skills.

Please join us on this exciting musical journey and help make the app better with your invaluable feedback and suggestions. Enjoy the journey to mastering trumpet fingering!
